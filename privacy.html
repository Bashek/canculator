We do not use third-party analytics or advertising tools.

We do not collect any information—personally identifiable or otherwise—through this App. The Calculator app operates entirely on your device. All calculations are performed locally, and no data is transmitted to any server or third-party service.

Since we do not collect any information, we do not process, sell, or share your information with any third parties.

3. Third-Party Services

We do not integrate or use any third-party services, APIs, or SDKs within this App. Therefore, no third-party has access to your data through our App.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you are a parent or guardian and become aware that your child has provided personal information in our App, please contact us. However, as stated, our App does not collect any personal or sensitive information from any user.

5. Data Security

Because no data is collected or transmitted by our App, there are no data security measures required beyond the normal security features of your device's operating system. Rest assured that your use of the App is entirely private.

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for operational, legal, or regulatory reasons.
