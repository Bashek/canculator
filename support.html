Before You Contact Us

Found a Bug?

If you've encountered a bug, it would be helpful to include:

  • • Steps to reproduce the issue
  • • What you expected to happen
  • • What actually happened
  • • Screenshots (if applicable)

Feature Requests

Have an idea for a new feature? Tell us about:

  • • The problem you're trying to solve
  • • Your proposed solution
  • • How this would improve your workflow

Performance Issues

When reporting performance problems, please include:

  • • Your device specifications
  • • When the issue started
  • • Any recent changes or updates
